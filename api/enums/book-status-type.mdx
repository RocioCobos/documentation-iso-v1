enum BookStatusType {
  OK
  KO
  ON_REQUEST
  CANCELLED
  UNKNOWN
  PENDING_COMMIT
}

### Values

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>BookStatusType.<b>OK</b></code>](#)

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>BookStatusType.<b>KO</b></code>](#)

>

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>BookStatusType.<b>ON_REQUEST</b></code>](#)

> The reservation was successfully completed; however, the product is currently unavailable, placing the reservation on a waiting list.
> It is the client's responsibility to confirm the status of the booking.

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>BookStatusType.<b>CANCELLED</b></code>](#)

> The status of the booking is cancelled.

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>BookStatusType.<b>UNKNOWN</b></code>](#)

> The reservation process itself may encounter problems, leading to an unknown reservation status.
> It is the client's responsibility to verify the booking's status.

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>BookStatusType.<b>PENDING_COMMIT</b></code>](#)

> The payment has been confirmed in provider s side, but is necessary make a commit in order to confirm the reservation.
