Payment type options.

```graphql
enum PaymentType {
  MERCHANT
  DIRECT
  CARD_BOOKING
  CARD_CHECK_IN
  PAYX
}
```

### Values

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>PaymentType.<b>MERCHANT</b></code>](#)

> The payment is managed by the supplier.

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>PaymentType.<b>DIRECT</b></code>](#)

> The payment is made straight to the actual payee, without sending it through an intermediary or a third party.

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>PaymentType.<b>CARD_BOOKING</b></code>](#)

> The payment is managed by the supplier. The payment is effectuated at the time of booking.

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>PaymentType.<b>CARD_CHECK_IN</b></code>](#)

> The payment is managed by the supplier and is made at the hotel during check-in.

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>PaymentType.<b>PAYX</b></code>](#)

> Payment management is handled by TravelgateX. Clients can use this payment method if they contract the virtual card
> creation (VCC) service. This service enables TravelgateX to manage payments that require card data in the booking process.
> There are two payment types that require card data: CARD_BOOKING and CARD_CHECK_IN. TravelgateX generates the card with the
> net price that the client needs to pay to the supplier.
