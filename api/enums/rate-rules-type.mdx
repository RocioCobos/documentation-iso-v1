Rate Rules refer to the specific terms and conditions that define the pricing, availability, and booking policies for rooms or accommodations at a hotel.

```graphql
enum RateRulesType {
  NORMAL
  PACKAGE
  OLDER55
  OLDER60
  OLDER65
  CANARY_RESIDENT
  BALEARIC_RESIDENT
  LARGE_FAMILY
  HONEYMOON
  PUBLIC_SERVANT
  UNEMPLOYED
  NON_REFUNDABLE
  MOBILE
  WARRANTY
  ESSENTIAL_WORKER
  NEGOTIATED
  CUSTOM
}
```

### Values

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>RateRulesType.<b>NORMAL</b></code>](#)

> NORMAL are options without RateRule.

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>RateRulesType.<b>PACKAGE</b></code>](#)

> The product can't be sold separately from another product attached to it, such as a flight.

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>RateRulesType.<b>OLDER55</b></code>](#)

> OLDER55 can only be sold to people who are 55 and older.

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>RateRulesType.<b>OLDER60</b></code>](#)

> OLDER60 can only be sold to people who are 60 and older.

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>RateRulesType.<b>OLDER65</b></code>](#)

> OLDER65 can only be sold to people who are 65 and older.

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>RateRulesType.<b>CANARY_RESIDENT</b></code>](#)

> CANARY_RESIDENT is applicable to Canary Islands residents only.

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>RateRulesType.<b>BALEARIC_RESIDENT</b></code>](#)

> BALEARIC_RESIDENT is applicable to Balearic Islands residents only.

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>RateRulesType.<b>LARGE_FAMILY</b></code>](#)

> LARGE_FAMILY is applicable to large families and is determined by each supplier.

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>RateRulesType.<b>HONEYMOON</b></code>](#)

> HONEYMOON is applicable to those who just got married and is determined by each supplier.

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>RateRulesType.<b>PUBLIC_SERVANT</b></code>](#)

> PUBLIC_SERVANT is applicable to public servants only.

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>RateRulesType.<b>UNEMPLOYED</b></code>](#)

> UNEMPLOYED applies to individuals who are without work.

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>RateRulesType.<b>NON_REFUNDABLE</b></code>](#)

> NON_REFUNDABLE is applicable to non refundable options.

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>RateRulesType.<b>MOBILE</b></code>](#)

> MOBILE are special rates confirmed by mobile phone.

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>RateRulesType.<b>WARRANTY</b></code>](#)

> WARRANTY is applicable to options with cancellation warranty.

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>RateRulesType.<b>ESSENTIAL_WORKER</b></code>](#)

> ESSENTIAL_WORKER are special hotel rates for essential workers.

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>RateRulesType.<b>NEGOTIATED</b></code>](#)

> NEGOTIATED are special rates offered only to specific buyers.

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>RateRulesType.<b>CUSTOM</b></code>](#)

> CUSTOM defines other rates.
